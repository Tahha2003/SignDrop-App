## Getting Started

### Accessing the Dashboard

1. Open your browser and go to your SignDrop URL
2. You'll see a login screen
3. Enter your password (default: `lawyer123`)
4. Click **Login**

> **Security Tip:** Change the default password immediately after first login by contacting your administrator.

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see:
- **Upload New PDF** button - Start a new signature request
- **Documents** table - View all your documents
- **Logout** button - Sign out securely

### Your Documents Are Saved

‚úÖ **All your documents are permanently saved**
- Documents persist even after you logout
- Available when you login again days or weeks later
- Survive server restarts and updates
- Only deleted when you manually delete them

## Uploading a Document

### Step 1: Select Your PDF

1. Click **Upload New PDF** button
2. Click **Select PDF Document**
3. Choose a PDF file from your computer
4. You'll see the filename and file size appear below

### Step 2: Place the Signature Box

1. Your PDF will appear on screen
2. You'll see a **red signature box** on the document
3. **Move the box:** Click and drag it to where you want the signature
4. **Resize the box:** Drag the small handles on the corners and edges
5. **Multiple pages?** Use Previous/Next buttons to navigate pages

> **Tip:** Place the signature box where you'd normally sign with a pen - usually at the bottom of the document.

### Step 3: Generate Signing Link

1. Once the signature box is positioned correctly
2. Click **Generate Signing Link**
3. Wait a few seconds for the upload to complete
4. A green box will appear with your signing link

### Step 4: Copy the Link

1. Click the **Copy** button next to the link
2. The link is now copied to your clipboard
3. You can now share it with your client

## Downloading Signed Documents

### Method 1: From Documents Table

## Troubleshooting

### "Upload failed" Error

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Make sure the file is a PDF (not Word, image, etc.)
3. Try a smaller PDF (under 10MB works best)
4. Refresh the page and try again

### "Unauthorized" Error

**Solution:**
1. You've been logged out
2. Click **Logout** then log back in
3. Try your action again

### Client Can't Sign

**Possible Issues:**
- Link expired (7 days old) - Upload again and send new link
- Document already signed - Check your dashboard
- Link was copied incorrectly - Copy and send again

### Can't Download Signed PDF

**Solution:**
1. Make sure the document status is "Signed" (green badge)
2. Check your browser's download settings
3. Try right-click ‚Üí "Save As" on the download button

### Signature Box Not Visible

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're on the correct page (use Previous/Next buttons)
2. The red box might be off-screen - scroll around
3. Refresh the page and try again

## Best Practices

### Security

- ‚úÖ Always log out when finished
- ‚úÖ Don't share your password
- ‚úÖ Use HTTPS (secure) URLs only
- ‚úÖ Download signed documents promptly
- ‚úÖ Delete old documents you no longer need

### Workflow Tips

1. **Name your files clearly** before uploading
   - Good: `Smith-Contract-2024.pdf`
   - Bad: `document.pdf`

2. **Send links immediately** after generating
   - Links expire in 7 days
   - Clients might forget if you wait

3. **Follow up** if not signed within 2-3 days
   - Check dashboard for status anytime
   - Your documents are always saved
   - Send a reminder email

4. **Download signed documents** when convenient
   - Documents stay in your dashboard
   - Download anytime, even weeks later
   - Keep local backups for your records

5. **Clean up regularly**
   - Delete old pending documents you no longer need
   - Archive signed documents locally
   - Your dashboard stays organized

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will my documents be there when I login again?

**Yes!** All documents are permanently saved. You can:
- Logout and login days or weeks later
- See all your documents exactly as you left them
- Check document status anytime
- Download signed documents whenever you need them

### What happens if the server restarts?

**Nothing changes!** Your documents are saved to disk, not just memory:
- All documents remain intact
- Signed PDFs are preserved
- Links continue to work (until they expire)
- You won't lose any data

### How long are documents kept?

Documents are kept **permanently** until you delete them:
- Pending documents: Forever (but links expire in 7 days)
- Signed documents: Forever (download anytime)
- You control when to delete documents

### Can I access old documents?

**Absolutely!** 
- All documents remain in your dashboard
- Scroll through the documents table
- Click on any document to view details
- Download signed PDFs from months ago
